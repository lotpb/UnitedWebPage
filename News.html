  •     About United Windows & Siding

    Since 1980, United Windows & Siding has specialized in commercial and residential exterior construction services for Long Island. United Windows & Siding residential services include siding, window, door and gutter installation and repair. Their commercial services include high-quality window systems installation and repair. United Windows & Siding is an insured contractor with a Home Improvement License in NY. To learn more, visit www.eunitedws.com or call 800-773-6633.


Customer Satisfaction is Every Employee's Goal:
We excel in customer satisfaction because we make an effort to identify each customer's specific needs before starting any project. An onsite foreman is then assigned to your job, which is responsible for seeing that the job is completed according to your contract.
