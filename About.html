Our Mission

Our mission is to determine and implement the right construction solution for each residential and commercial customer. We strive to provide fast and friendly construction services with the highest level of craftsmanship available, whether it is a simple repair or a complex re-siding operation.

Our Company

Founded in 1981 in Long Island NY, United Windows & Siding specializes in commercial and residential exterior construction services for New York, New Jersey, and Connecticut. United Windows & Siding has completed over 5,000 projects in that time and was recently recognized by The Town Crier as the seventh largest Siding contractor in the Long Island area and as the largest Siding contractor specializing in the residential market. United Windows & Siding has over 15 dedicated employees and is a fully licensed, bonded, and insured contractor.

Our People

Our employees are skilled, hard working, friendly professionals. They love what they do. And, they strive for ever-increasing customer satisfaction. Each employee participates in our SQE Points Program to ensure ongoing personal and professional growth. Our onsite foremen are well experienced at dealing with multiple projects and people - to ensure the success of the job and the satisfaction of the customer.

Our Projects

Jobs are monitored and visited by corporate officers to ensure that quality work is performed and that proper safety precautions are taken. Customers are given a week that their job will be completed within. Whether your job is large or small, United Windows & Siding will scale the crew to match your project and will complete all work in the agreed upon timeframe.
