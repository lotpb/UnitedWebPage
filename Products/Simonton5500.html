  • Introducing Simonton 5500 Vinyl Window

    Simonton Reflections windows feature the look and style of classic wood windows, with the strength, energy efficiency and durability of vinyl. In addition the durable fusion-welded construction and a variety of advanced, energy-saving glass packages and performance. From the smallest detail,the Simonton 5500 windows have been designed to increase energy savings, hinder weather penetration and provide low maintenance

Model 5500

  • Simonton 5500 Features

    The Frame and Sash are fused together to create a weathertight, one-piece unit, The triple stepped sloped sill design forces water away from the window, Overlapping and interlocking meeting rail virtually eliminates air and water infiltration where the sash meet. Dead-air chambers inside vinyl profiles create small, airtight pockets for increased insulation. Dual fin weather stripping creates a weather resistant seal around the operable sash. Coil spring balance system ensures easy sash movement and holds the window in any open position. Dual air locks allow window tostay partially open for ventilation with added security. ProSolar Low E Glass helps keep warm air in during winter and cool air in during summer.

  • Optional Features

    Specialy grids from flat, sculptured, brass, and shadow bevel-cut, White, Honey oak, Amber oak and Tan interior finishes, Full fiberglass screens available, Obscure glass
