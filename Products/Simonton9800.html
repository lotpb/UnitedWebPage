  • Triple Panes of glass

    The Triple-paned glass system starts with three panes of glass - a center pane of glass surrounded by two panes of double-strength glass. This provides increased strength while the center glass pane maximizes thermal performance and helps maintain inside temperatures at an ideal level.

  • Krypton Gas

    The triple-paned glass system contains a Krypton gas fill in two separate air spaces so energy is conducted at an even lower rate and the number of convection currents is further reduced.

  • Super Spacer

    The Super Spacer system employs energy efficiency even further with a non-metallic solid silicone foam that is placed within the frame so conduction and condensation rates are dramatically lowered.

  • ProSolar Architectural-grade, Soft Coat Low E Glass from PPG

    The triple-paned glass features four layers of ProSolar soft coat Low E glass which in warmer climates helps keep hot air out and cool air in. In cooler climates, it helps keep warm air in and cold air out. It also blocks about 60% of total solar energy while offering an exterior appearance similar to clear glass.

  • Double-strength Glass

    For increased performance, the triple-paned glass system features double-strength glass on both the inside and outside panes. Double-strength glass provides greater structural integrity and added strength while helping to buffer the intrusion of unwanted outside noise entering the home.
