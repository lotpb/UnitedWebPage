Vinyl Sliding Door

  • Simonton ProFinish Sliding Patio Door

    Open a room and bring more light into your world. Created from premium vinyl profiles that will never rot, warp or require painting, Simonton doors are virtually maintenance free. Plus, advanced features make the Simonton's door energy efficient, strong and safe, while various options help you create a look that complements your personal style.

  • Simonton Features:

    Fusion welded Panels to effectively stop air and water infiltration Reinforced with steel for extra strength
    Fin Seal Weatherstripping
    A Full Interlock system that overlaps and interlocks for a weathertight seal
    Double strength tempered glass for increased safety
    Multi-Point locking system
    External keyed lock that permits the door to be locked/unlocked from the outside
    Corrosion-resistant rollers and sill track
    Extruded screen frame provides added strength and rigidity
