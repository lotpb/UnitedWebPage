  • Introducing CertainTeed Monogram Siding

    Monogram was also the first siding designed to perform under high wind conditions through proprietary technology. The concept is based on a rolled-over nail hem design that creates a reinforcing spine.

CertainTeed Monogram Siding

  • Monogram is a comprehensive system of value-added features with unique benefits that combine to create a truly extraordinary siding.  Monogram was the first siding produced utilizing a direct transfer system from real cedar boards for natural woodgrain textures.  Combined with CertainTeed's unique Vinyl Carpentry concept, the look of true wood craftsmanship can be created with Monogram and decorative trim products.

  • Virtually maintenance free, never needs painting. Class 1(A) fire rating. Lifetime limited warranty. Works with traditional length Monogram panels. Long on Looks With traditional 12' panels, seams appear every two to three feet.  Monogram’s longer length panels can reduce seams up to 47%.

  • Monogram  has great benefits:

    Two Classic Styles - Two Length Choices: Double 4" clapboard, 16' 8" or 20'. Double 5" dutchlap, 16' or 20'. Heavy-Duty .046" Thickness. Low-gloss, rough cedar finish. Patented STUDfinder™ is designed for accurate and secure installation. RigidForm 180 technology has been tested to withstand wind load pressures up to 180 mph. CertiLock self-aligning, post-formed positive lock. 12 solid colors with a wide variety of coordinating trim
