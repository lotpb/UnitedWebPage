Alside Landscape Siding

  • Landscape provides beauty, quality, convenience and value at an affordable price. Its sophisticated nail hem design features staggered twin-nailing slots to deliver maximum panel attachment and a strengthening rolled top-edge to increase rigidity. Combined, these features help to ensure quality installations, even walls and straight course lines. And with its natural, beautiful wood look, Landscape rolls all the premium features into a competitively priced package.

  • Landscape Features

    Dutch lap and Clapboard profiles * Authentic grain pattern in distinctive natural cedar * Low-gloss finish for the appearance of freshly painted wood * Widest variety of trim, soffit and accent options on the market * Lifetime, transferable warranty*
