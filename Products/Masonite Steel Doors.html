Masonite Steel Entry Doors

  • Entry Doors FEATURES

    Masonite pinnacle steel entry doors offer premium features and benefits that offer the consumer so much value.  All Masonite pinnacle entry doors are primed in a durable white finish and can be painted any color to complement your home. The beautiful handcrafted decorative glass enhance the appearance of every home and reflect your own unique style. All Masonite pinnacle steel decorative glass lites are constructed from four panels of glass. The surface facing the outside of the home is made from strong , impact resistant tempered glass. Facing the inside of the home are two layers of annealed glass laminated together with a smash through resistant film
