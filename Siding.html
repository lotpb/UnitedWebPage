Siding

  • Charter Oak XL

    If details are important, youll be delighted with Charter Oak XL. These 16’8” panels are 40% longer than conventional siding panels. That means a cleaner, more attractive finished appearance with fewer overlaps.

  • Charter Oak XXL

    Bring out the beauty of your home with Charter Oak XXL reinforced premium vinyl siding. It’s more than twice as long as standard siding, 25' to be exact. That means significantly fewer overlaps if any at all. And with fewer overlaps to see, there’s much more beauty to appreciate.

  • CenterLock

    CenterLock features a patented mid-panel lock design that pulls panels tight to the wall and provides enhanced panel rigidity. The unique lock design delivers twice the holding power of ordinary vinyl siding. And the extra-thick panels mean straighter, smoother, more even walls. In wind resistance tests, CenterLock siding stayed locked in place at wind forces exceeding 150 miles per hour.

  • Odyssey Plus

    Odyssey Plus siding is everything you'd expect from a premium siding product, without the premium price. Extra panel thickness and an advanced lock design provide superior rigidity and holding power. And if you like choices, Odyssey Plus has you covered. With 4 profile options, available in 14 beautiful, timeless colors, and a full range of color-matching trim and accessories, it’s no wonder that it earned the name Odyssey Plus.

  • Landscape

    Landscape provides beauty, quality, convenience and value at an affordable price. Its sophisticated nail hem design features staggered twin-nailing slots to deliver maximum panel attachment and a strengthening rolled top-edge to increase rigidity. Combined, these features help to ensure quality installations, even walls and straight course lines. And with its natural, beautiful wood look, Landscape rolls all the premium features into a competitively priced package.

  • Pelican Bay Shakes

    Pelican Bay polypropylene shakes and scallops are among the most attractive, best wearing and longest lasting accent panels on the market, and are backed by one of the best warranties* in the industry.

  • MainStreet

    MainStreet offers consistent quality, good looks and is the ideal choice for homeowners and building professionals looking for value with the benefits of a premium panel.

  • Monogram

    Monogram's industry leading color palette and comprehensive system of value-added features and benefits create a truly extraordinary siding. Monogram continues to lead the way in innovation through many important industry firsts.

  • Cedar Impressions

  • Royal Crest

    Royal Crest is a highly durable vinyl siding that exceeds industry impact standards. It features a natural woodgrain finish and comes in a range of popular exterior shades. The latest Royal Crest innovation is a re-designed nail hem that provides an easier and more secure installation along with much higher wind resistance.

  • WoodLand 25

    Unlike standard vinyl siding, Royal Woodland 25' siding gives you a virtually seamless appearance. This results in a smooth, continuous finish with fewer interruptions and greater design possibilities.

  • Trim and Accessories

    Decorative Accents and Exterior Accents demonstrate our attention to every last detail. And it’s the details that deliver the look and performance of custom design. From gable vents to fluted corner posts, these decorative options are inspired by the beautifully handcrafted finishing touches. And thanks to the ColorConnect color matching system, you can be confident you’ll find the shade that matches, complements or contrasts with your siding perfectly.
